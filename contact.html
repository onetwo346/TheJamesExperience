Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit a question for a live Q&A?

You can submit questions through the live event page or during the live stream chat. I try to answer as many questions as possible during each session.

Do you accept book review requests?

While I appreciate the interest, my schedule is quite full. For review copies, please contact my publisher through the press inquiry form.

Can I request a signed book?

Yes! Signed copies are available through the books page. I also occasionally do signing events announced on the live events page.

How do I join the community forums?

Simply visit the Community page and create a free account. You'll have instant access to all discussion boards and features.

Are you available for speaking engagements?

I consider speaking opportunities on a case-by-case basis. Please use the contact form with "Collaboration Opportunity" as the subject.

When is your next book coming out?

Stay tuned to the blog and newsletter for announcements about upcoming releases. I always share news with my subscribers first!
